Empowering Visually Impaired Women in Kiminini

We provide vocational training, life skills, and opportunities for blind and partially sighted women to thrive and lead.

Our Focus Areas

Vocational Skills

Our workshops teach sewing, reusable sanitary pad production, and other skills for sustainable income and self-reliance.

Health & Hygiene

Tactile learning and guidance on menstrual hygiene and personal care empower women to lead healthier lives.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly production of reusable sanitary pads reduces waste and benefits both communities and the environment.

Community Impact

We foster confidence and leadership within the community, enabling women to mentor others and engage actively in society.
